## 4. Patient Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Here are the 10 most common questions asked by clinical patients, along with their professional medical answers:

### Q1: Is a hair transplant really painless?
Yes. At Dr. Paul's Clinic, we perform advanced QHT (Quick Hair Transplant) and FUE procedures using local anesthesia, ensuring you feel absolutely no pain or discomfort during the graft extraction or implantation.

### Q2: What is the cost of a hair transplant at Dr. Paul's Clinic?
The exact cost depends on the number of hair grafts required to cover your bald area. Our pricing is highly competitive and starts at very reasonable rates. We invite you to share your contact number so our medical counselor can provide a customized graft estimate for your scalp.

### Q3: How many days of recovery are needed after a hair transplant?
Most patients can return to light desk work within 48 hours. The tiny scabs from graft implantation heal completely in 7 to 10 days. Full, natural hair growth becomes visible in 6 to 9 months.

### Q4: Are hair transplants permanent?
Yes, absolutely. The hair grafts are taken from the donor area (usually the back of the head), which is genetically resistant to hair fall and male pattern baldness. The transplanted hair will continue to grow naturally for the rest of your life.

### Q5: What is the difference between PRP and i-PRF treatments?
PRP (Platelet Rich Plasma) uses your own blood platelets to stimulate follicles. i-PRF (Injectable Platelet Rich Fibrin) is the next-generation, 100% organic advancement of PRP. It forms a slow-release fibrin matrix that releases growth factors over a longer period, resulting in much faster hair regrowth and superior healing.

### Q6: How many sessions of PRP or i-PRF will I need to see results?
Usually, a course of 4 to 6 sessions (spaced 3 to 4 weeks apart) is highly recommended for optimal density and reduction in hair loss. Visible hair growth and thickening typically begin after the 2nd or 3rd session.

### Q7: Can the Korean Glass Skin Facial treat active acne?
No, the Korean Glass Skin Facial is designed to deeply hydrate, brighten, and refine skin texture for a smooth, glowing look. For active acne, our dermatologists recommend customized Medi-Facials, specialized Chemical Peels, or advanced fractional laser therapy first to clear the skin.

### Q8: What are the side effects of Glutathione IV Therapy for skin brightening?
Glutathione is a powerful natural antioxidant already present in the human body. When administered under clinical supervision at our clinic, it is highly safe, detoxifies your organs, and brightens skin tone without any major side effects.

### Q9: Does Laser Hair Removal cause burns or scarring?
No. We utilize state-of-the-art, FDA-approved, cooling-assisted lasers that selectively target melanin in the hair follicles. It is highly safe, virtually painless, and leaves the surrounding skin completely untouched and unburned.

### Q10: How does Fusion Homeopathy treat autoimmune hair loss like Alopecia Areata?
Alopecia Areata is an autoimmune condition where the body’s immune system attacks hair follicles. Our unique Fusion Homeopathy combines classical homeopathic medicine (which safely regulates the immune response from the inside) with advanced external cosmetic treatments to regrow hair naturally from the root.

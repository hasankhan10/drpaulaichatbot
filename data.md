## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### Q1: What is the cause of hair fall?
Hair fall can be caused by genetic predisposition (Androgenetic Alopecia), stress, nutritional deficiencies, or scalp issues like dandruff. We recommend performing our [Hair Stage Assessment](https://www.drpaulsonline.com/hair-stage-assessment) to help diagnose the root cause.

### Q2: What is QHT and is it permanent?
QHT (Quick Hair Transplant) is our premium painless transplant technology. It offers dense packing and natural results. Yes, it is permanent and backed by a **25-Year Warranty Support** because the donor hair is genetically resistant to hair fall.

### Q3: What is the difference between PRP and GFC?
PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) uses standard platelet release. GFC (Growth Factor Concentrate) is an advanced, next-generation treatment where growth factors are pre-extracted from platelets in a clinical lab, ensuring higher concentration, zero pain, and much faster results than standard PRP.

### Q4: How does Fusion Homeopathy treat Alopecia Areata?
Alopecia Areata is an autoimmune issue where the immune system attacks hair follicles. Our Fusion Homeopathy combines internal classical homeopathic medicines to balance the immune system, alongside advanced external regenerative therapies to regrow hair naturally.

### Q5: Can the Korean Glass Skin Facial treat active acne?
No, the Korean Glass Skin facial is for texture refinement and deep hydration. For active acne, our dermatologists recommend customized Medi-Facials, specialized Chemical Peels, or advanced fractional laser therapy first.

### Q6: How many sessions of PRP or GFC are required?
Usually, a course of 4 to 6 sessions (spaced 3 to 4 weeks apart) is recommended for visible density and hair fall reduction. Results typically begin showing after the 2nd or 3rd session.

### Q7: Which hairs are used for a hair transplant?
A hair transplant extracts healthy, permanent hair follicles from the patient's own donor area (typically the back or sides of the scalp). These follicles are genetically resistant to DHT (the hormone that causes pattern baldness), meaning they will continue to grow permanently in their new location.

### Q8: Will the donor area grow back or look empty after extraction?
No, the extracted hair follicles do not grow back. However, our expert doctors use advanced FUE/QHT™ micro-punch tools to extract grafts in a diffuse, staggered pattern. This leaves surrounding hair intact and ensures that once healed, the donor area looks completely natural and full without visible empty patches or scarring.

### Q9: Are the doctors at Dr. Paul's Clinic MBBS qualified?
Yes, absolutely. We follow a strict 100% doctor-supervised clinical treatment philosophy. All consultations, diagnostics, and critical steps of your hair or skin procedures are handled directly by qualified and registered MBBS doctors, MDs, or DNB dermatologists and trichologists. No critical medical procedure is led by technicians.

### Q10: I am scared about a hair transplant because of seeing weird/unnatural results on someone else. What makes Dr. Paul's different?
Unnatural results usually happen due to poor hairline design or incorrect grafting angles. At Dr. Paul's, we use advanced QHT™ (Quick Hair Transplant) technology which focuses on natural hairline alignment, high-density graft packing, and custom angling to match your natural hair flow. All of this is backed by an industry-leading **25-Year Warranty Support**.

### Q11: How is the QHT hair transplant virtually painless?
Traditional hair transplants can be uncomfortable during local anesthesia injections. We solve this by utilizing advanced **Dentapen painless injection technology** and ultra-fine micro-needles, which distribute the anesthesia gently and painlessly. The actual graft extraction and implantation are performed under complete local numbness, so you will feel no pain.

### Q12: Is a hair transplant surgical or non-surgical?
A hair transplant is a minimally invasive surgical procedure because it involves transferring live tissue (hair follicles) under local anesthesia. However, it is an outpatient procedure with no stitches, minimal downtime, and you can safely return home on the same day.

### Q13: Can a 74-year-old person get a hair transplant?
Yes! Hair transplants are highly successful for seniors, provided they are in good overall health and have a sufficient, healthy donor area (on the scalp or beard) to harvest grafts. We recommend booking a consultation to perform a medical check and scalp assessment.

### Q14: What is Advanced PRP and how many sessions are required?
Advanced PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) utilizes platelets from your own blood to release growth factors into the scalp, stimulating dormant follicles. Typically, 4 to 6 sessions spaced 3 to 4 weeks apart are recommended. For even faster, more concentrated, and painless results, we recommend **GFC (Growth Factor Concentrate)**.

### Q15: What should my husband do for beard hair breakage and lack of regrowth?
Beard hair breakage and patchy hair loss can be caused by infections, stress, alopecia areata, or nutrient deficits. We offer specialized beard therapies (including micro-injection therapies, GFC, and beard transplants). Please avoid self-medication; schedule a doctor-led consultation to identify the exact cause and get a customized prescription.

**Mytee 5%** is a clinical-grade Minoxidil solution that increases scalp blood flow to revive shrinking hair follicles in pattern baldness. It should be applied daily (usually 1 ml, once or twice a day) directly onto a completely dry scalp in the thinning areas, followed by a light finger massage. *Always state: "Product suitability depends on diagnosis."* and consult our doctor before starting.

### Q17: Can Hairfact M1B12 Plus be used in place of V1B12 Plus?
**Hairfact** is a gender-specific, cyclical nutritional therapy program. **M1B12 Plus** is formulated for male physiological requirements, whereas **V1B12 Plus** is tailored to female requirements. They contain different vitamin and mineral balances and should not be swapped without a doctor's recommendation.

### Q18: What is Exosome treatment for hair, and how does it benefit?
**Exosome Therapy** is a cutting-edge cellular therapy using nano-sized vesicles (exosomes) derived from stem cells. They are packed with active growth factors, signaling proteins, and coenzymes that trigger cellular repair and intensive follicle rejuvenation. It is highly effective for advanced thinning. Please consult us for customized pricing based on your scalp requirements.

### Q19: Does the Hairfacts supplement program help in hair regrowth?
Yes. Hairfacts is a cyclical vitamin and mineral therapy designed to correct internal nutritional deficiencies that cause hair thinning. While it strengthens existing hair and supports regrowth, patients with active pattern baldness achieve the best results by combining Hairfacts with clinical scalp treatments like GFC or i-PRF.

### Q20: Can genetic hair loss (Androgenetic Alopecia) be cured?
Genetic hair loss is coded in your DNA and cannot be permanently "cured," but it can be highly successfully **controlled and managed**. Treatments like GFC, i-PRF, and clinical minoxidil/finasteride can halt hair fall and regrow hair, while bald areas can be permanently restored with a **QHT™ Hair Transplant**.

### Q21: How does oral Minoxidil help, and is it a replacement for topical minoxidil spray?
Oral Minoxidil is a systemic prescription medication that stimulates hair follicles internally. It is highly effective, especially for patients with sensitive scalps or poor compliance with topical sprays. It is *not* a direct over-the-counter replacement and must only be taken under strict doctor prescription and dosage supervision.

### Q22: What is the process for removing moles and warts?
We remove moles and warts safely and painlessly using advanced **Radiofrequency (RF) ablation** or **CO2 Laser technology**. The procedure takes just a few minutes, is performed under local numbing cream, and leaves minimal to no scarring with fast healing.

### Q23: Do you offer electrolysis hair reduction, and what machine is used?
Yes, we offer professional electrolysis for permanent hair removal, which is particularly effective for white, grey, or fine hairs that lasers cannot target. We use advanced, international-standard clinical electrolysis machines designed for precision and safety.

### Q24: Does laser or electrolysis remove hair permanently?
Yes, both are highly effective. **Laser Hair Reduction** targets melanin in dark hairs and reduces hair growth by up to 90% painlessly over a series of sessions. **Electrolysis** targets individual hair follicles directly and is FDA-approved for 100% permanent hair removal (ideal for white/grey hairs or individual stubborn strands).
